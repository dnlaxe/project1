Learn English by Fixing Your Mistakes

Tired of robotic grammar checkers? Checkmate connects you with real people who help you improve step-by-step through personalized video corrections.

Step 1: Upload Your Writing

Essay, homework, speech draft — just drop it in. The more real, the better. Checkmates are here to help you with what matters most to you.

Step 2: Choose Your Checkmate

Browse expert checkers. See their specialties, styles, and prices. Choose someone you connect with.

Step 3: Get a Correction Video

Your Checkmate records a short video: they mark your writing and explain what to fix — and why. Watch, listen, absorb.

Step 4: Learn. Rewatch. Improve.

This isn't just feedback — it's a personal mini-lesson. Rewatch anytime. Grow your skill with every submission.

Why It Works: Backed by Research

  • Learners retain more when corrections are explained aloud — not just marked (Lyster & Ranta, 1997).
  • Watching + listening activates multiple parts of the brain, improving understanding (Paivio, 1986; Mayer, 2001).
  • Seeing a human checker “think aloud” boosts metacognitive skills and deep learning (Ericsson & Simon, 1993).
