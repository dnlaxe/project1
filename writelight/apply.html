Before You Apply

Please review the following requirements. When you're ready, you can begin your checker application.

Is your language open?

Before applying, check if your teaching language is currently open for applications.

Understand reliability standards

Checkers are expected to complete tasks on time. Learn what's expected before applying.

Have the right tools

You'll need a microphone, screen recording software, and the ability to annotate PDFs in browser.

Review payment process

Understand how and when you’ll be paid for completed feedback sessions.

Get familiar with the platform

Take a quick tour of the WriteLight interface to understand how tasks are assigned and completed.
